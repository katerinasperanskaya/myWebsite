intro

Hey, my name is Katerina Speranskaya and I am a professional musician. I’ve just moved from Dublin to Portarlington, Co. Laois. I don’t know anyone here, so please say hi if you’re from here! :)

I play piano, cello and organ, and I hold a Masters Degree in music performance. I have a broad range of experience from playing for weddings, to being a church organist, to playing with orchestras, to arranging music, to playing in trad sessions... Get in touch!

By the way, check out my awesome work.

about me

I graduated from TUD with a Masters Degree in Piano Performance (Accompaniment) in 2017, and since then I’ve been floating around and getting involved in things that come my way. In 2017 I played piano in a chamber music recital in the John Field Room, accompanying a Swedish cellist. In 2019 I played a solo gig all by myself at the German Embassy for an event attended by Mr. Michael D. Higgins. That same year I was the assistant accompanist for the International Conductors School in Limerick. Last year I played cello as a part of the support act for Muireann Nic Amhlaoibh’s gig at the Dock Theatre in Carrick On Shannon. This year I was one of the main accompanists for the Newpark Festival, and I also travelled down to Limerick to be the repetiteur for the Opera workshop, funded by the Arts Council.

I have won prizes in the Feis Ceoil music competitions, as well as other national classical music competitions, and I have participated in international piano competitions. I also hold an Associate Degree in cello performance from Trinity College London.

I am always busy with things to play for every week – there’s always a pile of new music to be learnt on my piano!
